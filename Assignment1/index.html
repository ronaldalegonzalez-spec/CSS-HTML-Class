About the Song

"No Pole" is a song by American rapper and singer Don Toliver. It was released through Cactus Jack and Atlantic Records as the first track from the deluxe edition of his third studio album, Love Sick, on February 28, 2023. Toliver wrote the song with producers Cardo and DJ Fresh, alongside Dougie F. The song gained mainstream popularity over a year and a half after its release due to its use on the online video platform TikTok.

The central theme of “No Pole” revolves around a lifestyle of indulgence, power dynamics, and personal relationships. Don Toliver narrates his experiences with a woman who is moving away from her previous life as a stripper, a shift symbolized in the chorus. He offers a glimpse into his own world, where money, control, and a sense of entitlement intertwine with his interactions. The song mixes playful confidence with a touch of vulnerability, exploring themes of loyalty, transactional relationships, and the tension between old habits and new desires.

About the Artist

Caleb Zackery Toliver, known professionally as Don Toliver, is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter from Houston, Texas. He is signed to Cactus Jack Records, Atlantic Records, and We Run It Entertainment. Toliver gained initial widespread recognition for his guest feature on Travis Scott's song "Can't Say" from the album Astroworld (2018).

Early Life

Toliver was born on June 12, 1994, in Houston. His father was also a rapper and was part of the Swishahouse movement in the early 2000s. Toliver began releasing music in 2017, reportedly using a Playstation Eye Camera to record his early work.

Career

In May 2017, Don Toliver released his debut mixtape, Playa Familia, which featured fellow Houston artist YungJosh93. He gained local traction with this release. His manager, Cozy Colby, persistently worked to get Toliver's music to Travis Scott's team.

On August 2, 2018, a day before the release of Travis Scott's Astroworld, Toliver released his second mixtape, Donny Womack. The mixtape included tracks like "Diva", "Diamonds", and "Backend". His feature on Scott's "Can't Say" from Astroworld significantly boosted his popularity. In 2019, he released singles such as "Best You Had" and "Back Up" (featuring Wiz Khalifa). He was also prominently featured on the JackBoys collaborative album JackBoys (2019), on songs like "What to Do?" (with Travis Scott) and "Gang Gang" (with Sheck Wes, Travis Scott, and Luxury Tax).

On February 23, 2023, Toliver released his third studio album, Lovesick, followed by a deluxe version on February 28. The album included the viral TikTok song "No Pole" and featured artists like Toro y Moi, Kali Uchis, Teezo Touchdown, Future, and Justin Bieber.

His fourth studio album, Hardstone Psycho, was released on June 14, 2024. It also achieved significant success with viral tracks such as "Tore Up" and "Bandit". The album featured collaborations with Future, Kodak Black, and Charlie Wilson.

Toliver has also been featured on other notable albums including SOS by SZA, Heroes & Villains by Metro Boomin, Music to Be Murdered By by Eminem, and Red Moon in Venus by Kali Uchis.
